## **Training in Art and Tailoring**

<br/>

---

<br/>

The Tailoring training course is an environment where refugees and locals gain skills in sewing different items. This knowledge empowers them to be able to work or start their own business.

<br/>

The Main focus areas of this project include:

1. To equip women and the youth with marketable skills for economic self-sustainability, in Art tailoring, Entrepreneurship, Book keeping.

2. To provide the beneficiaries with an opportunity or a platform to use the acquired skills to generate income.
