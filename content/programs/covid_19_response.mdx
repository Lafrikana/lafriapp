## **Covid-19 response program**

<br/>

---

<br/>

The aim of this program is to provide and distribute relief packages of food, sanitary and hygiene items to urban refugees and needy Kenyans living in Nairobi.

<br/>

The Main focus area of this project is:

1. To distribute foods and sanitary items to the needy in the community.
